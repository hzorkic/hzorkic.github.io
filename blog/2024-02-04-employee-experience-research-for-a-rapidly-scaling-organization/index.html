Employee Experience Research for a Rapidly Scaling Organization

Background

In the ever-evolving landscape of innovation, Texas Inventionworks (TIW) stands as the epitome of a pioneering force in engineering and education at The University of Texas at Austin. As a dynamic “skunk works” initiative, TIW redefines the conventional engineering education paradigm. From its modest roots in a 1,700-square-foot lab, TIW has burgeoned into a multifaceted program. The expansion into the EER marked a pivotal moment, propelling TIW into exponential growth across technology, community outreach, and an expanding workforce. Through this TIW Employee Experience project our goal was to take a pause to do two things: (1) evaluate how we are currently doing and (2) identify where & how we should improve. By doing so, we aim to launch TIW into new frontiers of innovation, to foster an employee experience that mirrors the spirit of creativity and excellence that defines our mission, and to positively shape the future of engineering education.
